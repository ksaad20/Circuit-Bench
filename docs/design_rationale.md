# Circuit-Bench Design Rationale

## Motivation

Electrical circuit design increasingly relies on machine learning for prediction, optimisation and automation. However, there is currently no widely adopted, standardized benchmark that enables fair and reproducible comparison of AI methods across diverse circuit design tasks.

Circuit-Bench addresses this need by providing a common benchmark framework for electrical circuit design research.

## Research Gap

Current evaluation practices are often characterized by:

- Small, task-specific datasets
- Inconsistent evaluation metrics
- Limited reproducibility
- Lack of standardized benchmark tasks
- Difficulty comparing methods across publications

These challenges hinder objective assessment of AI methods for electrical circuit design.

## Design Goals

Circuit-Bench was designed to achieve the following objectives:

1. Standardized evaluation
2. Reproducible experimentation
3. Broad circuit coverage
4. Simulator interoperability
5. Extensible benchmark design
6. Open research infrastructure

## Benchmark Principles

Circuit-Bench follows six guiding principles.

- Reproducibility
- Transparency
- Fairness
- Extensibility
- Accessibility
- Scientific rigor

## Target Users

Circuit-Bench is intended for:

- Machine learning researchers
- Electrical engineers
- Electronic design automation researchers
- Benchmark developers
- Academic institutions
- Industrial research laboratories

## Expected Scientific Impact

Circuit-Bench aims to provide a standardized benchmark that supports reproducible evaluation of AI methods for electrical circuit design. By establishing common datasets, tasks and evaluation protocols, the benchmark seeks to improve comparability across studies and facilitate future research.

Its long-term impact will depend on community adoption and continued expansion.

## Current Limitations

Circuit-Bench is an evolving benchmark.

Future work includes:

- Expanded benchmark datasets
- Additional benchmark tasks
- New simulator integrations
- Community benchmark submissions
- Public benchmark leaderboards
